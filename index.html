Why UX Design and Usability Testing? Where do you see yourself within this nexus of UX design and product development?

As a writer I constantly think about the usability of my documents. When it comes to professional writing I am consciously thinking about how to create the most usable document. It is from this that I thought it might be useful to actually understand usability in a broader sense. This will allow me to move beyond just writing in my work and expand my future potential. However, I have a more personal relationship with the concept of usability, especially when it comes to written text. As a poet I often play with the overall usability of my art to create meaning and purposeful confusion. So this class will also provide me with a greater understanding of just how to manipulate usability to create a variety of outcomes.
